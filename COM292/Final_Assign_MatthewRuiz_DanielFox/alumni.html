Alumni Of The Month

Brother John Smith, 1984

Congratulations To The New Alumni Class Of 2016

     The alumni class of 2016 has 18 brothers leaving us and moving on to bigger and better things. All though they may be gone either back home or somewhere new but one thing is for sure, they will always have a brothers in West Chester. Of the graduating there are 5 guys that truly made their mark. First of the class was 2015's past Richie Shcafer, Richie was always the go to guy about anything. There was never something he wasnt able to give his words of wisdom about. Next is Ryan Brown, aside from being one of the people that could always make you laugh, he set an example of what it means to be a brother.Moving on, Structually speaking two of the main pillars that kept us up have also graduated;John McCain and Drew Schier. There was never anything these two couldn't handle, when in need or question Johnny Mac or Schier always had a solution. Lastly, Ryan Light also known as the general. Light was many things for this fraternity from Social Chair, to Homecoming Chair,and Pledge Master the guy was the swiss army knife of brothers. That being said aside from these five all of our Seniors will be missed dearly and we look forward to seeing them soon.
